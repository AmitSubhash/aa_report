No-Outcome Students by Year

Methodology & Data Notes

Denominator

The denominator is drawn from DSS (Decision Support System) — IU Bloomington graduate degree awards (degree levels 8 and 9, checkout status = AW). Each student appears once, keyed to their most recent (maximum) cohort graduation year.

What counts as a "true match"?

A Lightcast record is a "true match" only when MATCH_STATUS == True. Having a row in the Lightcast file is not sufficient — many rows have MATCH_STATUS = False, meaning Lightcast could not confidently match the student to an employer.

What counts as an employment outcome?

  • AA employment: any Academic Analytics row with non-blank ENTITYNAME or POSITION
  • Lightcast employment: true match (MATCH_STATUS == True) AND non-blank COMPANY_NAME or TITLE_NAME
  • PSEO is not employment — it is engagement / career services interaction

Which source gives the most coverage?

Lightcast has the broadest row-level reach but only ~30–40% of rows yield true matches. AA is a supplemental source with ~10–12% student coverage. PSEO covers ~14% for engagement.

Where does coverage fall apart?

The single largest gap is Lightcast row-only-no-true-match — students who appear in Lightcast but whose MATCH_STATUS is False. This is the dominant no-outcome reason.

Year coverage caveats

  • Earlier cohorts (pre-2010) have lower match rates across all sources
  • 2025 data may be incomplete (partial year)
  • AA is snapshot-based, not continuous annual. Years 2021–2024 may appear sparse due to AA's data vintage

International students

Students flagged as "US Nonresident" in DSS are classified as international. International students typically show lower match rates across all vendor sources.

Bloomington denominator

Yes. DSS is filtered to IUBLA (Indiana University Bloomington). Lightcast may include other campuses at the row level, but the denominator is always Bloomington graduates via DSS JOIN_ID linkage.

Bucket definitions (disjoint, precedence order)

  1. AA_EMPLOYMENT — AA has non-blank entity/position (highest priority)
  2. LIGHTCAST_EMPLOYMENT — LC true match with non-blank company/title
  3. PSEO_ONLY_OR_ENGAGEMENT — PSEO row exists but no employment outcome
  4. NO_OUTCOME — none of the above
